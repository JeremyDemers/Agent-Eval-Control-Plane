# Code of Conduct

AgentEval welcomes constructive participation from people of all backgrounds and experience levels.

Be respectful, specific, and technically honest. Critique ideas and behavior rather than people.
Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, and publication of private information
are not acceptable in project spaces.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and participation that violate these standards.
Serious or sensitive conduct concerns should be reported privately through the contact method in
[`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md), with enough context for a fair review.

By participating, you agree to follow these expectations in issues, pull requests, discussions, and
other project interactions.
